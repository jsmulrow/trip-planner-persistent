# RESTful Trip-Planner API Overview

## Days

Get all days
	GET, /days

Get one day
	GET, /days/dayId

Make a new day
	POST, /days

Update a day
	PUT, /days/dayId

Delete a day
	DELETE, /days/dayId

## Attractions

Get a day's hotel
	GET, /days/dayId/hotel

Get a day's restaurants
	GET, /days/dayId/restaurants

Get a day's things to do
	GET, /days/dayId/thingsToDo
